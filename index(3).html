Setup Definitions
📉
Oversold Bounce
RSI < 30 at or near support. Wait for RSI to curl up past 35 before entry. Target previous swing high.
🚩
Bull Flag
Strong impulse leg followed by tight consolidation on lower volume. Enter breakout of flag high with a stop below the flag low.
⚡
MACD Crossover
MACD line crosses above/below signal line. Best in trending markets (ADX > 25). Confirm with price action.
📈
MA Breakout
Price closes above 50-day EMA after being below. 200-day as long-term bias filter. Strong when RSI 50-65 range.
🔄
Support Flip
Old resistance becomes new support after a breakout retest. High-probability entry; stop below the reclaimed level.
〰️
Mean Reversion
Price stretched beyond 2 ATR from the mean. Works best in ranging/low-ADX conditions. RSI divergence adds confidence.

Technical Indicators
〰️
RSI (14)
Momentum oscillator. Overbought >70, Oversold <30. Best used at support/resistance confluences. Divergence is a leading signal.
⚡
MACD (12/26/9)
Trend-following momentum. Signal line crossovers and zero-line crosses confirm trend changes. Histogram shows momentum acceleration.
📊
SMA 50 / SMA 200
Key support/resistance. Golden cross (50 > 200) = bull signal. Death cross = bearish. Price vs 50 SMA = intermediate trend bias.
📐
EMA 20 / EMA 50
More reactive than SMA. EMA 20 as dynamic support in uptrends. Use on daily chart for swing trade entries, 4H for timing.
📏
ATR (14)
Average True Range = volatility measure. Size positions and set stops using ATR multiples (1.5–2.5×). Expanding ATR = momentum.
💧
Volume / VWAP
Breakouts on 1.5× avg volume are significant. VWAP = intraday fair value. Below VWAP = bearish, above = bullish intraday bias.
Fundamental Metrics
💰
P/E Ratio
Price relative to earnings. S&P 500 avg ~17-20x. High P/E = growth priced in; watch for compression. Use forward P/E for growth names.
📈
EPS Growth
Year-over-year earnings growth. Sustained >15% growth justifies premium valuations. Acceleration matters more than absolute level.
🏛️
P/B & P/S Ratio
P/B for banks/value stocks. P/S useful for high-growth companies without earnings. Compare within sector, not across.
📉
DCF Valuation
Discounted Cash Flow. Most sensitive to growth rate and discount rate assumptions. Use as range, not point estimate. 20% margin of safety.
🔢
Debt / EBITDA
Leverage ratio. Below 2x is conservative; above 4x warrants caution in rising rate environments. Watch interest coverage ratio.
🏆
Insider Activity
Cluster buying by multiple insiders = strong bullish signal. Form 4 filings via SEC EDGAR. Single sells are less meaningful.
